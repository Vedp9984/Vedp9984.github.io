Achievements

==========================================================

National Level JNVST Exam (5th Class)

Cracked the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test (JNVST) securing a place in Navodaya Vidyalaya.

Divisional Talent Search Exam (7th to 10th Class)

A consistent performer, achieving top 10 ranks in the Divisional Talent Search Exam. Secured 1st rank in class 10, receiving a bike as a prize.

Center of Excellence (COE) Selection (Class 10)

Selected for the Center of Excellence program by Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti in class 10. Started JEE preparation at COE Allahabad in 12th, 97%ile in JEE Main and qualifying for JEE Advanced without coaching.

Swachhta Sarthi of the Year 2020-21

In class 8, submitted a startup idea under the Waste to Wealth mission of Niti Ayogya. Our idea was selected, and we were honored as Swachhta Sarthi of the Year 2020-21. Received an award certificate signed by the Cabinet Minister and funding from Invest India to work on the idea.

Meeting in Delhi

Worked on the startup idea for a year and were invited to Delhi for a meeting near Rashtrapati Bhavan. The meeting involved discussions with investors for funding expansion. Although unable to attend, the experience was invaluable.

IIIT Hyderabad Admission (SPEC Exam)

Aced the SPEC (Special Channel of Admission) exam for admission to IIIT Hyderabad. Exceptional interview performance secured a spot in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE).
